How This Works

Suppose you have an amazing product that you are trying to sell to a business that you know will benefit from your product. Even then, your buyer may present various "objections" to purchasing your product.
 
As a seller, your job is to "handle the objection." There are several good strategies for handling objections, such as the "Listen, Acknowledge, Explore, and Respond" approach. After you have learned a strategy, use this website to verbally practice handling objections. It will randomly generate one of different objections, and give you seconds to answer it.
